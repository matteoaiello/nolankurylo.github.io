  • About Me

During my first coop work term, I decided to start learning a new language and build a mobile app that others could use. This project was one that I did on my own time outside of work.


This app serves as an IOU system for groups of people. The idea for this app came from living in a house with some schoolmates and the troubles of sharing purchases. For example, if myself and my three roommates were to hop in an cab ride to go downtown, that would have costed about $8. It makes the most sense to have one person pay for the cab and the rest of us pay that person back afterwards. However, who wants to dig around for a toonie or send a $2 e-transfer?


With this app, IOUs between groups of people can be tracked and acculumated to a point where there is enough money owed to pay the other person back.
